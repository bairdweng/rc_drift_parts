## Installation Guide

### Step-by-Step Installation Process

1. **Remove Existing Mounts**: Carefully remove traditional body clips and posts
   ![Installation Step 1](https://i.ebayimg.com/images/g/azQAAeSwheNpcMLv/s-l1600.webp)

2. **Clean Mounting Surfaces**: Ensure surfaces are free of dirt and debris
   ![Installation Step 2](https://i.ebayimg.com/images/g/pAAAAeSw2KdpcMAM/s-l1600.webp)

3. **Install Magnetic Bases**: Secure magnetic bases to chassis mounting points
4. **Attach Magnetic Plates**: Position magnetic plates on body interior
5. **Test Alignment**: Verify proper alignment and magnetic hold
6. **Fine-Tune Height**: Adjust mounting height for optimal clearance
   ![Final Installation](https://i.ebayimg.com/images/g/sAoAAeSwe85pcMAP/s-l1600.webp)

### Installation Tips

- Use thread locker on mounting screws for permanent installations
- Test magnetic strength before final assembly
- Ensure proper clearance between body and chassis components
- Consider using multiple mounts for larger bodies
